About France Alliance Corporation

France Alliance was founded by FlyToulouse, quickly joined by Fr_Space, Nice Clouds & Houston.
It exist since 16/02/2020

Started as an experiment, a few members started to join in the same short period of time. As soon as the now-founders started talk with each other, something, somewhere, click and one of them created a Discord server...
And then, everything went super-fast: They realised they had time, they had 2 devs, 1 designer, a charismatic leader who had the perfect right-hand-man. Roles were distributed, a logo was made, sheets were created and money was being made...
Quickly an ad was launched and they received many many applications. So much that they had to make a decision:
They voted the only that it was right to do. They voted the creation of the comitee and of another alliance...
Quickly full, they had to widen again. Joined by 2 new members, the comitee created the Instagram account, the Twitter account and the now-legendary DDRC.
Followed a more calm period, where they polished the server, build a Discord bot, experimented new things and project, to provide the best experience to members. And as of today, you have only see a part of what is planned...
